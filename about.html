Who We Are

Born in the Halls of शाकुन्तलम्

We are Shakuntalites — a name that carries the weight of seven unforgettable years, the warmth of lifelong friendships, and the pride of two graduating batches.

This page is a digital tribute — a living memory album — crafted for every student who walked through the iron gates of our school and called it home. From Class 6 to Class 12, the journey shaped us in ways no textbook ever could.

Here you will find galleries of our events, farewell memories, teacher tributes, and a wall of wishes from hearts that refuse to forget. This is not just a website — it is our farewell, immortalised.

Two batches. Batch 2024–25 and Batch 2025–26. Different years, different stories — same unbreakable spirit.

What We Stand For

The Pillars of Our Bond

The five-fold education of Banasthali Vidhyapeeth — a philosophy that shaped every Shakuntalite.

01 🏃
Physical Education

A healthy body is the foundation of everything. Sports, yoga, and discipline on the field taught us strength, teamwork, and the will to keep going.

02 🎨
Aesthetic Education

Music, art, and culture nurtured our creative souls. From dance to painting, we learnt to see beauty in the world and express it fearlessly.

03 🔬
Practical Education

Hands-on learning connected textbooks to real life. Practical skills, workshops, and life lessons that no classroom alone could ever teach.

04 📚
Moral Education

Values, integrity, and character — the quiet lessons that outlast every exam. We were taught not just how to succeed, but how to be good human beings.

05 🧠
Intellectual Education

Curiosity, critical thinking, and the love of learning. From science to literature, our minds were sharpened to question, explore, and grow.
