01
📜 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how शाकुन्तलम् विद्यालय, Banasthali Vidhyapeeth, Rajasthan – 304022 ("the School") collects, uses, and protects information provided by visitors to this website.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. This policy applies to all visitors — students, parents, teachers, and guests.

In brief: We do not sell your data, display advertising, or share your information with third parties except as described in this policy.

02
📊 Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate this website. This may include:

  • Name & Section — when you submit a wish or message on the Wishes wall.
  • Written messages — the text of your wish or farewell note, displayed publicly on the Wishes wall.
  • Contact form inputs — name, email address, batch year, subject, and message submitted via the Contact page.
  • Basic analytics — anonymised page-view data may be collected via third-party tools to understand how the site is used.

We do not collect passwords, payment details, government IDs, or sensitive personal information.

03
💬 Wishes & Messages

When you submit a wish or farewell message through the Wishes Wall, your name, section, batch year, and message are:

  • Stored in a Google Sheets database connected to this website via a secure Google Apps Script.
  • Publicly visible on the Wishes wall to all visitors of the website.
  • Retained indefinitely as part of the permanent memory archive of the school batch.

By submitting a wish, you consent to your name and message being publicly displayed. Please do not include personal phone numbers, email addresses, or any information you would not wish to make publicly visible.

If you wish to have your message removed, please contact us at [email protected] and we will action your request within 7 days.

04
🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

This website may use browser Local Storage and cookies to remember your preferences and settings. These are stored on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

  • Local Storage does not expire automatically — it persists until you clear your browser data.
  • It is used solely to store user preferences relevant to your browsing session.

Third-party services (such as Google Analytics) may set their own cookies to collect anonymised usage statistics. You can opt out via your browser's privacy settings or a browser extension.

05
🔗 Third-Party Services

This website integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — to load the beautiful typefaces used across the site. Google may log the request.
  • Google Apps Script / Sheets — to store and retrieve wishes submitted on the Farewell Wall.
  • YouTube — embedded videos are loaded from YouTube. YouTube's own privacy policy applies when you play a video.
  • Google Analytics (if active) — anonymised page view data may be collected to help us understand usage patterns.

Each of these services is governed by its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them independently. We do not control the data practices of third-party providers.

06
⚖️ Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your information:

  • Right to access — You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion — You may request removal of your wish, message, or any other data you have submitted.
  • Right to correction — If your name or message was recorded incorrectly, you may request a correction.
  • Right to object — You may object to your data being used or displayed at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 7 working days.

07
🔄 Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the website evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top of the sidebar. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. If you disagree with any changes, please discontinue use of the site and contact us to have your data removed.

08
✉️ Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact the school directly.
